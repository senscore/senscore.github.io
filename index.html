About SensCore AI

SensCore AI Lab is dedicated to discovering and sensing core values in complex systems and applying cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence to improve the quality of human life.

Our multidisciplinary team bridges the gap between physical systems, advanced sensors, and sophisticated AI models to solve real-world problems.

Innovation

Pioneering new approaches in AI and sensing technologies.

Collaboration

Working together with industry and academic partners.

Research Areas

Physical AI & Multi-modal System

Integrating physical intelligence with multi-modal data streams for comprehensive system understanding.

VLM & VLA

Advancing Vision-Language Models and Vision-Language Action for intelligent agents.

Sound Quality & STT-TTS

Enhancing voice quality and developing robust Sound-to-Text and Text-to-Sound technologies.

HCI & Biomedical Engineering

Improving Human-Computer Interaction and applying AI to biomedical engineering challenges.

Sensor System & Data Science

Developing advanced sensor networks and utilizing data science for actionable insights.
